About Me

Welcome to My Page! I am Sangwon (Eric) Shin, a dedicated researcher in biomechanics and exosuit development, with a strong passion for advancing stroke rehabilitation care. My work focuses on human movement analysis, gait biomechanics, and innovative passive exosuit designs to improve mobility and rehabilitation outcomes. With a background in biomechanics, I strive to bridge the gap between engineering and clinical applications. My goal is to develop accessible, patient-friendly technologies that enhance rehabilitation strategies for stroke survivors and older adults. This website serves as a platform to showcase my research, experience, and contributions to the field. If you're interested in collaborations, research insights, or clinical applications, feel free to explore my work or reach out!
